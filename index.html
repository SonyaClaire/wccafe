⛎ About the 13-Sign Zodiac
The astronomical (sidereal) zodiac tracks the actual position of the Sun against the constellations. NASA notes the Sun passes through 13 constellations — including Ophiuchus, the Serpent Bearer. This shifts all sign dates from the traditional system. Both systems have value — choose what resonates.
